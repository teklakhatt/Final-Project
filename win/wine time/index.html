about

Wine bar of Dakishvili “Wine Time” will be situated in the historical part of town Telavi. A guest visiting us will have possibility to taste and buy only the best wines that are made in Georgia. The quality control will be carried out by means of our own wine laboratory and supervision of a professional wine maker, member of our team. Permanent wine presentations and events will be held in the wine bar, which will extend our customers’ knowledge in winery and also farther develop culture of wine consumption. The Bar “Wine Time” is designed for both tourists interested in Georgian wine, as well as for local customers, who want to taste and buy high quality wine, moreover for the ones who wish to extend their knowledge in the wine field. We will use a specially made application to evaluate wine quality in our bar, which represents innovation for Georgia. This evaluation will be monthly provided to wine makers, which will give them the option to find out how much does the customers like their wine. Thence, they will get a customer’s feedback to consider the next technological development steps in the winery .
